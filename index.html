About Sarafa

In today's rapidly evolving financial landscape, Sarafa platform help financial institutions to stay ahead of the curve. Sarafa is designed to meet the unique needs of these organizations, providing them with the tools and functionality they need to manage their businesses efficiently and effectively, while also delivering exceptional customer experiences.

Users and Permission Management

Create users, roles and groups. Flexible control roles to restrict access to certain functionalities and information.

Fees & Limitations Management

Manage fees to be automatically collected based on different factors, and set up limitations for accounts and transactions.

Treasuries Management

Manage treasuries to efficiently handle cash flow, liquidity, and investments.

Multi-currencies Accounts

Transact in different currencies and manage foreign exchange operations.

Clients Management

Facilitate personalized financial services and streamlined client interactions.

Multiple Reports and Statements

Gain insights into financial performance, track transactions, and comply with regulatory requirements.

Trading Assets

Place trading orders, manage currency pairs and exchange rates.

Sub-Organizations Support

Effortlessly manage sub-organizations through the main organization

Integration

Unlock new possibilities and simplify your workload by integrating with Sarafa API.
